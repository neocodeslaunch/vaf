What We Do

Programs built around what communities truly need

Four interconnected pillars — each program designed with the communities it serves, not just for them.

Pillar 1

Healthcare

Access to essential healthcare is a right, not a privilege. VAF works to bring basic health services, awareness programs, and medical support to communities that have historically been underserved by the formal health system.

  • Health awareness camps and community outreach
  • Support for maternal and child health
  • Partnerships with local clinics and health workers

Pillar 2

Education

Quality education is the most powerful tool for breaking cycles of poverty. VAF runs learning support programs, drives access to schools and materials, and supports children and youth through their educational journey.

  • After-school learning support and tutoring programs
  • School supply drives and scholarship support
  • Digital literacy and skills development
  • [Additional program details to be added by VAF team]
Pillar 3

Environment

Healthy communities need healthy ecosystems. VAF's environmental programs focus on conservation, sustainable practices, and building awareness around the environmental challenges that directly affect our partner communities.

  • Tree plantation and green cover restoration drives
  • Waste management and cleanliness awareness
  • Sustainable agriculture and kitchen garden training
  • [Additional program details to be added by VAF team]
Pillar 4

Development

Social and economic empowerment completes the picture. VAF's development programs focus on building livelihoods, supporting women's empowerment, and creating pathways to economic independence for individuals and families.

  • Vocational training and skills development
  • Women's empowerment and self-help group support
  • Livelihood and micro-enterprise support
